Jaiden Carter

Jaiden Carter is a 21 year old artist from Chicago. He then moved to Ireland when he was 7. Ever since he was little, he loved to draw on the walls of his house and he eventually turned his passion into a reality.

Jaiden now goes to college in Waterford Institute of Technology and is studying Design. He hopes to move back to America after his course in college and paint the streets of his hometown. He promises to continue to submit his future photographs of his works to us to put on our website.

Suki Ito

Suki Ito is a 28 year old artist from Tokyo, Japan. She moved to Dublin in 2012 and attended Trinity College to study Art. She found out about our gallery through the Michelangelo exhibition.

She has curated many exhibitions in our gallery over the past 9 years and is a very well-known artist around both Dublin and Waterford. For her next collection, she hopes to create a traditional Japanese woodblock printing collection. We look forward to having her back!

Mark Murphy

Mark Murphy is a 23 year old local artist from Dunmore East, Waterford. From a young age, he always loved nature and he loved going out with his parents down to the beach and painting the boats on the harbour.

He chose Art for the Leaving Cert and went on to do it in Waterford Institute of Technology in the form of Visual Design. He is now a proud member of the paint house. and is family to us!
