Welcome to the paint house.

the paint house. is located in 23 John's Hill in Waterford and is one of the most famous galleries in Ireland. In 2013, one of their profound members, Jessica Chitas, curated an exhidition using Michelangelo's works including The Pieta and it became the first gallery to host his works in Ireland.

Since Lockdown started on 13th March 2020, we decided to bring our gallery to the online world and host virtual exhibitions so we created a virtual gallery where you can view our featured works of the week from our featured artists.

Jessica Chitas, CEO
